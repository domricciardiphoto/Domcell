Apple AirPods Pro with Wireless MagSafe Charging Case (2nd Generation)

AirPods Pro (2nd generation) with MagSafe Charging Case (USB-C) bring you cutting-edge audio technology small enough to fit in the palm of your hand. Superior audio quality comes standard with the upgraded H2 chip, and adaptive EQ works in real time to automatically adjust audio to sound its best. These earbuds can switch between Noise Cancellation and Transparency Mode, allowing you to block unwanted noise or hear the world around you whenever necessary, and touch controls for the volume offer easy adjustments.

The MagSafe charging case delivers exceptionally fast charging, with five minutes in the case providing enough charge for up to an hour of listening or talk time. On a full charge, you'll enjoy up to 6 hours of listening time and up to 4.5 hours of talk time. If you've misplaced your MagSafe case, the built-in U1 chip with Precision Finding will help you locate it. These earbuds are dust, sweat and water resistant, and four sizes of silicone ear tips are included to ensure anyone can find their perfect fit
