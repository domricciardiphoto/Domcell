<h2 class="t-h4-style" style="color: rgb(3, 70, 148);">Why is Antivirus Software Necessary?</h2>

<p>Total Defense antivirus software offers strong defense against the many dangers you could potentially face online. The most prominent things you might stumble upon are malware and viruses. Though these can come in many forms, they can all cause harm typically by stealing your information, or damaging your <a href="/laptops/">laptop </a>or PC to the point where it may be borderline unusable. Phishing attacks are also very common, mostly in the form of malicious emails with the intent of having you reveal sensitive information. They can come in the form of phony work emails, or even fake messages about family members. You might also find yourself exposed by a data breach, where access is gained to your personal information through a variety of nefarious means. Any of these unfortunate things can happen to anyone, and even the most seasoned internet expert can fall victim to a clever phishing scam.</p>
